Wed 22 Apr 26

The Avener

+ Vhyce

French producer with piano roots and an electronic soul

Tristan Casara, or The Avener, is a DJ, producer and musician who began studying classical piano at the Conservatory of Nice at the age of five. He released his first album ten years ago, The Wanderings of the Avener, from which the single Fade Out Lines (a cover of a Phoebe Killdeer song) became a hit worldwide that sold 1.5 million copies.

That success made him one of the new stars of the electronic music world and the accolades soon followed: triple platinum in France, then platinum and gold in more than ten other countries, plus a Victoire de la Musique in 2015. All followed by a tour of the World’s most prestigious clubs and festivals.

In 2020, he released his second album Heaven, containing singles like Beautiful (with Bipolar Sunshine) and Under The Waterfall (with M.I.L.K). This year, he revealed Lunae Veritatis. Clearly, he is ready to return to the origins of where his career began: the love of dance music and DJ culture.
