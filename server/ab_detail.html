Tue 21 Apr 26

Stijn plays Prince - 10 years after

+ DJ Sasha Van Der Speeten

Tribute on the tenth anniversary of the passing of ‘His Royal Badness’

STIJN has never hidden the fact that he loves listening to Prince. Since the start of his career in 2001, a Prince song would find its way into his setlist every now and then. After the passing of this exceptional musician, STIJN filled various venues (De Casino, the StuBru Club at the Lokerse Feesten, Charlatan and Club K) with his unique tribute.

Don’t expect When Doves Cry or Kiss. STIJN plays the lesser known, sometimes even unreleased Prince tracks. A unique tribute that takes you through the wide-ranging oeuvre of the musical jack-of-all-trades from Minneapolis. A setlist for the true fans, who know that Prince made more than Purple Rain and 1999.

For the tenth anniversary of the passing of this icon, STIJN returns to his musical roots in Brussels, where he sold out several AB Clubs with his STIJN plays Prince. This exceptional edition promises to be a hot & sweaty night. This ain’t over yet … These Are The Days Of Wild.

Warming up before and cooling down after the concert will be done to the selection of DJ Sasha Van der Speeten, music journalist for De Morgen and Humo, music fan and Prince connoisseur.

  • Funk / Soul / R&B
